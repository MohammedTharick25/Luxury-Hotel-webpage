Restaurants & Bars

Grand Horizon boasts an exceptional collection of dining venues, from our Michelin-starred restaurant to the vibrant rooftop bar, each offering distinctive culinary experiences.

Aurora

Contemporary French
Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Lunch: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Dinner: 6:30 PM - 10:30 PM

Our Michelin-starred signature restaurant offers an exquisite journey through contemporary French cuisine, crafted by Chef lise Laurent. The seasonal tasting menu showcases the finest local ingredients with innovative techniques.

Dress Code: Smart Elegant

Horizon Terrace

Mediterranean
Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Lunch: 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Dinner: 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Al fresco dining at its finest, our rooftop terrace offers stunning panoramic views alongside a menu inspired by the sun-drenched flavors of the Mediterranean. Perfect for sunset cocktails and relaxed dining.

Dress Code: Smart Casual

Sky Lounge

International
Open: 10:00 AM - 1:00 AM

The social heart of the hotel, Sky Lounge offers all-day dining with an international menu, afternoon tea service, and evening cocktails with live jazz performances Thursday through Saturday.

Dress Code: Casual Elegant

Cloud Nine Bar

Cocktails & Light Bites
Open: 5:00 PM - 2:00 AM

Perched on the 42nd floor, Cloud Nine offers craft cocktails with breathtaking city views. Our mixologists create innovative drinks using premium spirits and house-made infusions.

Dress Code: Smart Casual
